Vision

The vision for Moorhouse is to be the premier consultancy recognized for delivering excellence in programme leadership for our clients' organizational transformations. We plan to achieve this by:

  • Providing exemplar, collaborative support to our clients in order to improve the delivery of their programs and contributing to the development of their internal programme management capabilities.
  • Attracting, developing and retaining talented and passionate consultants through considerate, staff-focused policies and behaviors.
  • Building and growing a profitable practice in order to provide client security and the requisite rewards and opportunities for the owners and staff of the business.

Values

Our values underpin the behaviour of the individuals within our team and describe how we do business. Moorhouse believes that our values should apply to all of our activities and not just how we engage with our clients. We aim to be consistent in the way we conduct ourselves with clients, business partners, employees and even competitors.

Integrity and accountability We believe that integrity and accountability are fundamental to consulting. At Moorhouse, we do what we say we are going to do and we don't believe consulting is ever about 'passing the buck'.

Collaboration We believe that how we do things is as important as what we do, so our approach is always collaborative in relation to working alongside our clients and together as a firm. Our experience is that working closely as a team always produces better results.

Sense of humour and perspective At Moorhouse, we make sure we take our work seriously but never ourselves. Amidst the pressure of hard work, a sense of humor and perspective is never far away. Take a look at our life@bernalandco.com microsite to find out more.

Positive pragmatism We take great pride in a positive 'can do' approach to our work. This positive attitude is, however, tempered by realism and honesty. We seek always to over-deliver on our commitments and this requires professional confidence and a constructive manner in defining realistic promises.
